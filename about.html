About Me

Personal Details

I've got experience doing projects for many years, so I know a lot about software development, even though I'm studying electrical engineering at UC San Diego. I've built full stack web applications in React and Python, including backend APIs as well as frontend GUIs. I've written hardware applications in C for the Raspberry Pi as well as Arduino and Arduino-based boards, and have some experience tinkering with TI and ARM-based microcontrollers. If you need anything, feel free to shoot me an email.

Email Me

Many people ask me why I study electrical engineering rather than computer science, and that's because I have greater opportunities and can realize greater potential when I am able to teach myself computer pgoramming. I have rows and boxes of electronics in my room, and you can almost always find me doing something which I love.

Recently, I've taken a keen interest to hacking, exploitation, and penetration testing. I like experimenting with electronics at the hardware level to see if I can find weaknesses or bugs that might allow me to exploit their flaws, and so if you would like to help me with such a project, feel free to shoot me an email as well.
